## Features ##

- A menu bar app for Mac.
- A simple Todo app.
- Manage tasks and subtasks.
- Drag & drop to reorder items.
- Export to a text file.
- Font customization.

## Requirements ##
- macOS 14.6 or later

## Privacy ##

- No personal data is collected.
